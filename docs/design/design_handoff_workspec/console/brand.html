Brand.

A spec OS for product builders. The brand is the voice of a fastidious senior engineer who happens to be cheerful — precise, dry, never floral. The wordmark is set in monospace because the product is.

01Wordmark

All-lowercase, monospaced, no logo glyph. The cursor is the only flourish — a 9×14 block in --accent. It signals "ready to type" and ties the brand back to the cold-start screen.

02Voice

Three principles. They're constraints, not aspirations.

Principle 01

Precise over polite.

Say what's true, not what's nice. "Fix the loop" beats "Address the issue with the authentication retry sequence." Never apologize for the user's mistake; never inflate small wins.

Yes
"3 notes look duplicated. Group them?"
No
"I noticed some potentially related items…"
Principle 02

Show your work.

The agent narrates what it's doing as it does it. Tool calls are visible. Reasoning is short but legible. Users earn trust by watching, not by being told.

Yes
→ tool: searchArtifacts(theme:"auth")

Principle 03

Density is respect.

Our users see twelve things at once and prefer it. Don't pad. Don't hide. Don't gate behind tooltips. If they need a key, give them a key — but show the door.

03Color story

Near-black canvas. Off-white ink. One hot accent (Heat). One cool agent color (Mint). Everything else is gray. Color carries meaning, never decoration.
