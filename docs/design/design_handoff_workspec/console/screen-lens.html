04Behavior

Switching

Toggle is always present, top-right of canvas. L cycles forward (L → D → B → L). ⇧L backward. The transition crossfades artifacts and lets edges morph in 280ms (--d-slow).

Persistence

Lens choice is per-board, persisted in URL. Sharing a board link includes the lens — colleagues see what you saw. The agent respects the active lens when answering: ask "what depends on auth-api?" in deployment lens, get services; ask in logical lens, get features.
