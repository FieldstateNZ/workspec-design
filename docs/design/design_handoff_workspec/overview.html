03

Pick & mix guidance

Recommendation

Direction B (Terminal) for the product surface — your users are technical, density rewards expertise, the dark canvas keeps focus on artifacts. Direction C (Atelier) for marketing — slower, considered, builds trust. Direction A (Paper) is the spirit of the wireframes; it's beautiful but probably too warm for daily use.

Caveats
  • This is a v0.1 — names, hex values, and component APIs are negotiable.
  • The screen set is the same across directions but copy varies a little to suit voice.
  • Motion is defined as principles + a small token set; build out as needed.
  • Marketing surfaces are sketched, not finished; shipping copy still TBD.
