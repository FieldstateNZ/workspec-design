Why Paper earns the pitch

Where Terminal asks the user to read like a console, Paper asks them to read like a draftsman. Same data model, different proposition: every artifact has provenance — drawn-by, dated, drafted-vs-final — and the agent appears as pencil annotations that solidify on accept. Sales motion: design-led teams, founders who care about craft, anyone fatigued by sass-tech UI.
