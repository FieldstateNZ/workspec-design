01 · How it works

One graph for research, structure, and work.

Notes become features become slices become tickets. Every artifact has provenance — drawn by, dated, drafted-or-final. Drafts stay legible. Versions stay legible. Work happens in the open.

01

Discover.

Drop notes, paste tickets, import a backlog. Atlas pencils the connections — you decide which ink to keep.

02

Structure.

Logical lens, deployment lens, both. Drill into containers. Promote drafts to ink. Supersede on the canvas.

03

Ship.

Boards live inside the canvas. Tickets keep their sources. Engineering gets context, not just a one-line summary.

WorkSpec is the first product tool where the AI feels like a junior I want to work with — visible, attributable, legible. It drafts. I decide.
— Maya R · Head of Product · Faro
