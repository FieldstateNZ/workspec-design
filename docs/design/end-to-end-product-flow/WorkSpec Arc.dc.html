Question 05 · Test vs reconciliation

Two different things — don't collapse them

Test is the terminal stage of one element's arc. Reconciliation — drift between the spec and the linked repo — is a continuous loop over the whole board, forever, keeping the spec honest after things ship. Here's the distinction made explicit, then where drift surfaces.

Test — per element, terminal
A chip on the element's own arc track. Belongs to one thing. Runs once it's built; the last stop on that element's journey.
Reconciliation — whole board, continuous
Not a stage. A standing loop comparing every shipped element against the repo. Drift can hit something that finished its arc months ago.
